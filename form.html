How to Fill out our data collection form

  1. Identify and mark the sex of the purple martin (so you can indicate which box they go to)
  2. Check the nesting box the purple martin flys to
  3. Click the boxes of all the behaviors observed. (multiple clicks are allowed)
  4. Submit your observations
